Discussion: Identity Online
The internet is incorporated in every part of our daily life. Due to this, it is interesting to see the benefits and disadvantages of the internet. The podcast discussed how the internet can create connections but, in some cases, we don’t know how real these connections are. The internet also has a habit of not contextualizing and giving credit to where and what it was inspired from. Seeing how many of these trends online are using cultural appropriation yet is deemed to be acceptable in society (with no credit) just shows how damaged the internet is. Finally, the talk on censorship really made me think of all the ways people’s freedoms are being taken away from them when their voices are censored online all around the world.
