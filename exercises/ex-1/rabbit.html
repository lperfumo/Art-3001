Evie the Rabbit's Home



You pass through to Evie the Rabbit's old home. It is cold in the room. Evie seems to have

left some of her things during the move. You trip over what seems to be a twig but it's really

too dark to see anything very well. You become a little nervous and prefer to keep on

moving. You can go to Elsie the raccoon's home to check in. You can keep Roman the armadillo

company. You can go to see Finn the skunk since they are home. You can go into your

new home to look around. You can go over to the empty home of Sawyer the mole.
