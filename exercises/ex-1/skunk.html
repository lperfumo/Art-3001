Finn the Skunk's Home



As you walk into Finn the skunk's home, you feel a sense of warmth that you have been

looking for. You feel relaxed.


"Hello there. I hope you are enjoying your new home. We all hope that you feel welcome

here. We all take up a role here in the Napeteccas burrow. I am known for taking care of

the young ones so let me know if I can ever help out."


You thank Finn for his kindness. Although you have a temptation to stay, you don't want

to intrude much longer. You can go to Roman the armadillo's home. You can go to

Willow the prairie dog's home to see if she has returned. You can go into your new home.

You can go to Evie the rabbit's old home which looks pitch black compared to Finn's home.
