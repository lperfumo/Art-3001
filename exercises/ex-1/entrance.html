Community Burrow



As you walk in, there is a bulletin board across the room that displays the town map. You look

down and see a variety of tracks from different animals. As you look around the room,

there seems to be three passages. Amara the badger seems to be home since you hear

chatter. You smell something wonderful coming from Roman the armadillo's burrow.

Elsie the raccoon doesn't seem to be home but it wouldn't hurt to check. All paths will

take you to your new home.
