Roman the Armadillo's Home



As you walk through the passage, you realize that you are hungry. There is an amazing smell of food

as you get closer. You walk in to see a lot of baked goods around Roman.


"Hello neighbor. I hope you are finding your way through our burrow well. If you want to grab a bite

you are free to take anything you would like. As you can tell, I love eating sweet treats."


You take a crumb from what seems to be a muffin and head on your way. You can head to the

community burrow to get directions. You can pop in to Elsie the raccoon's home and look around.

You can go visit Evie the rabbit's home but it seems like she doesn't live in Napeteccas anymore. You

can head down to Finn the skunk's home to see how he are doing. You can go to Willow the prairie dog's

home. You can go to chat with Amara the badger.
