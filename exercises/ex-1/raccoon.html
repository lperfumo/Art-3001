Elsie the Raccoon's Home



As you pass through the tunnel you feel like you are intruding into a stranger's home. However,

you see a picture of Elsie in the room and seem to recognize her. As you walk around, you hear

something coming from the other side of the walls. You assume its from one of your neighbor's

homes. You become a little uncomfortable and realize that you should keep on moving forward.

You can go back to the community burrow. You can head to what used to be Sawyer the mole's

home. You can go see Evie the rabbit's home which seems to be completely dark. You can visit

Roman the armadillo in his home which smells tempting.
