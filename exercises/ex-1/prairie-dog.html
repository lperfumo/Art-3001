Willow the Prairie Dog's Home



As you enter the room, you see just how frantic Willow must be with work. There are papers

and dirt lying everywhere. You pick up a couple papers off the floor. You wonder how much

stress she must be dealing with. You decide that you will join the next community council

meeting and volunteer to help out to take some pressure off of her hands. You can go check

what kind of food Roman the armadillo has in his home. You can go talk to Amora the badger

about your new home. Your can go to Ayden the gopher's home. You can go to Finn the skunk's

inviting home.
